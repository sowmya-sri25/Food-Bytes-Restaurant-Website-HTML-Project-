About Breakfast

Breakfast is the first, essential meal of the day eaten in the morning to break the overnight fasting period, boosting energy and metabolism. It often includes coffee, tea, eggs, toast, cereal, or bacon. A traditional "Full English breakfast" is a hearty, warm meal featuring bacon, sausages, eggs, beans, mushrooms, and toast.
